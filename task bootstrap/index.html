What is artificial intelligence in simple words?

Artificial intelligence is the science of making machines that can think like humans.

What is AI to explain?

The simulation of human intelligence processes by machines, especially computer systems.

What is AI used for?

AI is the ability of a machine to display human-like capabilities such as reasoning, learning, planning and creativity.
